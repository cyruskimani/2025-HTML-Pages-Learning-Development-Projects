Grilled Chicken Recipe

This juicy grilled chicken recipe blends smoky flavors with a hint of spice, perfect for any occasion. Easy to prepare, it delivers tender, mouthwatering results every time.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken breasts or thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken in a shallow dish and pour marinade over chicken, ensuring each piece is covered, then refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the Grill: Heat grill to medium-high and lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  4. Grill the Chicken: Place chicken on the grill and cook for 6–8 minutes per side until the internal temperature reaches 75°C / 165°F.
  5. Rest and Serve: Remove from heat, let rest for 5 minutes.Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
